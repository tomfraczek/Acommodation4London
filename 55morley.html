House Descreption:

A large beautiful carpet-floored Victorian house split into two storeys. There is one bathroom and one of the rooms is en-suite. It also has a big living room with a nice TV and a lovely kitchen. Share this amazing house with 7 other friendly people.

A 15-minute walk from Leytonstone Station, Central London is a short tube ride away and easily accessible also by 24-hour buses.

Buses: 69, 97 and N26

The area is very nice and convenient with shops and restaurants nearby and there is a cricket pitch a stone’s throw away!

There is a garden with a barbecue where you can chill after work and have a drink with your flatmates.

The rent includes ALL BILLS: council tax, electricity, gas, water, fast internet and a cleaner coming regularly for common areas, so the house is very clean.
